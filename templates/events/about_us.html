Connecting Our Engineering Community

kvents is the premier event management platform designed specifically for Khwopa Engineering College. We bridge the gap between event organizers and participants, making it easier than ever to discover, register for, and manage events within our vibrant academic community.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive platform that enhances the event experience at KhEC, promoting active participation, knowledge sharing, and community building among students, faculty, and staff.

Our Vision

To become the central hub for all academic and extracurricular activities at KhEC, fostering a vibrant campus culture and strengthening our engineering community.
